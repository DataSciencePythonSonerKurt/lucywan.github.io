My First Video Game

Last week, I finished my first video game, "Save Mr. Onionhead", for my game design class at my high school. The game was made with Stencyl, and although the game was not my first time working with Stencyl, it was my first time developing a stencyl game from scratch. In the game, an onion, Mr. Onionhead, is trying to escape an underground kitchen without touching the knife characters. The player uses the arrow keys to play. The inspiration for the game came from playing Super Mario games when I was younger. Although this game is very basic, I hope it leads to my turning point to creating more advanced games in the future.

To play "Save Mr. Onionhead", please go to my portfolio tab and look under "Game Development".
